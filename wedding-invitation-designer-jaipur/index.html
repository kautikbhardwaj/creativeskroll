Digital wedding invitations — what they are and why they matter

A digital wedding invitation is a professionally designed video or animated graphic created to be shared digitally — via WhatsApp, Instagram stories, and email. Unlike printed cards that get lost or forgotten, a digital invitation arrives directly in your guests' hands, looks stunning on any screen, and can include clickable links to venue maps, RSVP forms, and wedding websites.

In 2026, most Jaipur families send both — a beautifully designed printed card for close family and elders, and a digital invitation for all other guests. This gives you the best of both: the tradition and formality of print with the reach and convenience of digital.

A well-designed digital invitation gets shared. When guests receive a beautiful animated invitation, they share it — on WhatsApp groups, Instagram stories, and family chats. Your wedding announcement becomes its own small event.

Why choose a Jaipur designer for your wedding invitations

Jaipur has a centuries-old tradition of visual artistry — from the intricate block printing of Sanganer to the miniature paintings of the old city. A Jaipur-based designer understands the visual language of Rajasthani weddings, the balance between tradition and contemporary elegance, and the specific needs of families planning events in the Pink City and surrounding region.

Creativeskroll's founder Sanchita Bhatia has designed motion graphics and brand identities for events across Jaipur. Wedding invitation design brings together all of these skills — typography, motion, colour, and storytelling — into one of the most personal and meaningful design projects imaginable.

The design process

  1. Brief via WhatsApp. Share your wedding details — names, dates, venues, events, and any design preferences (colours, style, motifs). Send reference images if you have them.
  2. Design concept. We develop an initial concept showing the visual direction — colours, typography, and design language — for your approval before production begins.
  3. Production. The full invitation is designed and animated based on the approved concept.
  4. Review and revisions. You review and request any changes. We refine until it's perfect.
  5. Delivery. Final files delivered via WhatsApp or email — ready to share immediately.
