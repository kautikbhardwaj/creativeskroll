Common questions

Everything you need to know

How do I get started?
Simply send a WhatsApp message or use the contact form. We will have a brief conversation about your project, timeline, and goals — and then provide a custom quote. No lengthy forms or complicated processes.
How long does a project take?
It depends on the scope. A logo design typically takes 7–14 days. A complete brand identity takes 2–4 weeks. Social media packages are delivered on an agreed monthly schedule. We discuss timelines before starting every project.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes — we work with clients worldwide. We have delivered projects for businesses in India, UAE, UK, US, Canada, and Australia. All project communication and file delivery happens remotely with no issues.
Will I own the final designs?
Absolutely. Upon final payment, full ownership of all design files transfers to you. We provide source files (AI, EPS, PSD) alongside all export formats — you are never locked into working with us.
How many revisions are included?
We do not put arbitrary limits on revisions. We work until the design is right for your brand. Our process is built around collaboration, not transactional exchanges — the goal is always a result you are genuinely proud of.
What information do you need to get started?
A brief description of your business, your target audience, any design references you like (and don't like), and your timeline. The more context you provide, the better the outcome. We will guide you through the rest.
