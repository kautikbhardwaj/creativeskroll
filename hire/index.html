FAQ

Common questions

How much does logo design cost?
Logo design starts from ₹8,000 for a professional, original logo with all vector files and full ownership. Brand identity packages start from ₹15,000. All prices are fixed — agreed before work begins, no surprises on the final invoice.
Do you work with clients outside India?
Yes — all project work is done remotely. Briefs are shared via WhatsApp or email, concepts presented as PDF, and files delivered digitally. We have worked with clients in the UAE, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and across Europe.
How long does a project take?
Logo design: 7–14 working days. Brand identity: 2–4 weeks. Social media design: 3–5 days per batch. Timelines are confirmed in the project brief before work begins.
What files do I receive?
All vector source files (AI, EPS, SVG), PNG exports with transparent background, PDF exports, colour variants (full colour, black, white), brand colour codes in HEX/RGB/CMYK, and font information. Everything you need for any application — print, digital, or signage.
Do I own the final design?
Yes — 100%. All intellectual property and source files transfer to you on final payment. No licensing arrangements, no ongoing fees, no conditions. The design is yours completely.
