Why motion graphics matter for your brand in 2026

Instagram, LinkedIn, and YouTube all now heavily favour video and motion content in their algorithms — which means a brand that only posts static images is fighting with one hand tied behind its back. Motion graphics give your brand presence in video feeds without requiring a camera, a studio, or an actor.

A well-crafted logo animation, a branded reel, or an animated social post communicates professionalism and attention to detail that static images cannot match. And because most small businesses and startups in India are still posting only static content, motion graphics immediately differentiate your brand.

What software do you use for motion graphics?

Adobe After Effects for complex animations, Adobe Premiere Pro for video editing, and Adobe Illustrator for creating motion-ready assets. All output is delivered as standard MP4 files compatible with all platforms.

How long does a motion graphics project take?

Logo animations: 5–7 working days. Brand reels: 7–10 working days. Animated social posts: 3–5 working days per batch. Wedding invitation videos: 5–7 working days.

Do you provide music and sound design?

Yes — royalty-free music selection and basic sound design is included in reel projects. For projects requiring original sound design or voiceover, we can coordinate this as an add-on.
