The logo design process

  1. Discovery brief — We ask you targeted questions about your business, audience, competitors, and brand values. The more we understand, the stronger the concepts.
  2. Research phase — We research your industry, analyse competitors, and identify design directions that are both differentiated and appropriate for your market.
  3. Concept development — We develop 3–5 original logo directions, each based on a distinct strategic insight from the research phase.
  4. Concept presentation — Concepts are presented as a PDF with rationale explaining the thinking behind each direction.
  5. Refinement — You select a direction and we refine it through unlimited revision rounds until it's exactly right.
  6. Final delivery — All files delivered in a clearly organised folder. Full ownership confirmed.

How long does logo design take?

7–14 working days from brief to final delivery. Initial concepts are presented within 5–7 days, followed by refinement and final file delivery.

Do I own the logo after the project?

Yes — 100%. All intellectual property and source files transfer to you on final payment. No licensing fees, no conditions.

What if I don't like any of the concepts?

This has not happened — but if initial concepts miss the brief entirely, we discuss, re-brief, and develop new directions. We do not stop until you are genuinely happy with the result.
