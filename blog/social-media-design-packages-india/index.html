Social media design packages in India —
what to expect and what to pay

By Sanchita BhatiaJune 15, 20256 min read

If you have been searching for a social media designer in India, you have probably noticed that pricing varies enormously — from ₹2,000 per month to ₹50,000 per month. What explains this range? And more importantly, what should a business like yours actually be paying for?

This guide breaks down social media design packages in India by tier, explains what each level delivers, and helps you decide where to invest based on your business goals.

Social media creatives — by Creativeskroll, Jaipur

What a social media design package typically includes

Before comparing prices, it helps to understand what you are actually buying. A social media design package from a professional designer typically includes a set number of post designs per month across your chosen platforms (usually Instagram and LinkedIn), designed consistently with your brand identity.

Better packages also include story designs, carousel posts, cover images, and occasional ad creatives. The best packages include a set of branded templates you own — so you can create simple posts yourself between the designer's deliveries.

Social media design pricing tiers in India — 2025

Basic tier (₹3,000–₹8,000/month): 8–12 post designs per month, minimal customisation, template-based work. Suitable for businesses that need a baseline of consistent content but do not have a strong brand identity to work with.

Standard tier (₹8,000–₹20,000/month): 12–20 post designs per month, fully customised to your brand, includes stories and carousels, one revision round per design. This is the most popular tier for small businesses and startups in India.

Premium tier (₹20,000–₹45,000/month): 20–30+ designs per month, strategic content planning, ad creatives, animated posts, unlimited revisions. For businesses running active paid campaigns and needing high-volume creative output.

One-time vs monthly: Many businesses start with a one-time branded template set (₹8,000–₹15,000) and then manage their own content using those templates. This is a cost-effective approach for early-stage businesses.

What to look for in a social media designer

Beyond price, there are several things to evaluate when choosing a social media designer in India. First, look at their portfolio — do the brands they have worked with look consistent across posts, or does each post look like it was designed in isolation? Consistency is the hardest skill to master in social media design.

Second, ask about their process. A good designer will ask about your brand guidelines, target audience, and content goals before producing a single design. If they start designing immediately without asking questions, expect generic results.

Third, clarify ownership. All design files — including source files — should belong to you on delivery. Never work with a designer who retains your files.

What should a complete social media design brief include?

To get the best results from any social media designer, come prepared with your brand colours and fonts, your target audience description, examples of posts you admire, your monthly content themes or promotions, and your Instagram handle. The more context you give a designer, the better the output.

Is it worth paying more for a specialist?

Yes — with an important caveat. A specialist social media designer who has worked extensively in your industry will produce better results faster than a generalist. If you run a restaurant, working with a designer who has a portfolio of food brand social media work means you get designs that communicate your food's quality immediately. Experience in a specific industry is worth paying for.
