Print design cost in India in 2026 — brochures, menus, flyers & more

By Sanchita BhatiaApril 20267 min read

Print design remains essential for Indian businesses despite the digital shift. Menus, brochures, business cards, posters, and packaging all require professional print-ready design that digital tools like Canva consistently fail to produce correctly. This guide covers exactly what professional print design costs in India in 2026.

Why print design requires a professional

Print design has technical requirements that digital-first tools do not handle well. Resolution (300 DPI minimum for print, vs 72–96 DPI for screens), colour mode (CMYK for print, vs RGB for digital), bleed areas, crop marks, and font embedding are all essential for professional print output — and all things that Canva and similar tools handle inconsistently or not at all.

A beautifully designed menu or brochure that is set up incorrectly for print will come out blurry, colour-shifted, or cut incorrectly. A professional designer delivers print-ready files that go directly to the printer with no technical issues.

What every print design project must include

  • 300 DPI resolution — minimum for professional print quality
  • CMYK colour mode — not RGB; colours look different in print without correct colour mode
  • 3mm bleed on all sides — ensures no white edges after cutting
  • Crop marks and registration marks — guides for the printer's cutting equipment
  • Fonts embedded or outlined — prevents font substitution at the printer
  • PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4 format — the standard print-ready PDF format accepted by professional printers

Frequently asked questions

How much does brochure design cost in India?

A 4–8 page brochure costs ₹5,000–₹18,000 from an experienced designer. Larger brochures of 8–16 pages cost ₹10,000–₹30,000. Prices vary based on complexity, number of pages, and whether copywriting is included.

What file format should I ask for from my print designer?

PDF/X-1a is the safest standard print-ready format, accepted by all professional printers in India. Also request the source file (AI or InDesign) so you can make future edits.
